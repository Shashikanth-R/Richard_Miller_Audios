Meet Our Founder

Albert Nathan

(Managing director)

Albert Nathan, the visionary behind Richard Miller & Audios, has over 10+ years of experience designing and building immersive home theatres and luxury entertainment rooms. His deep understanding of acoustics, visual design, and smart automation sets a benchmark in the home theatre industry.

Under his leadership, the studio has transformed hundreds of living spaces into extraordinary cinematic experiences, combining technology and design artistry.
